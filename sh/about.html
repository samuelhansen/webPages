ABOUT

Samuel Hansen is the Director and Podcast Producer at ACMEScience.com, mathematician, and general internet gadabout. He created and hosted the mathematical interview show Strongly Connected Components, went on and on about fights from the history of science in Science Sparring Society, and covered breaking mathematical news with Peter Rowlett on Math/Maths. His biggest creation is the twice Kickstarted series Relatively Prime which tells the best stories from the mathematical domain.

On a more personal note Samuel lives in the wonderful city of Detroit. He spends his free time riding his bikes all around the city, playing(and watching) soccer, and listening to as many podcast as he can at double speed.
